Help Veterans In Need

I hope you enjoyed getting to know me. I became a Marine to change who I was. For me the change was good, but others were less fortunate. 6.5% of Americans are Veterans, of that total many suffer from the effects of war. I have seen The Wounded Warrior Project in operation first hand and the work they provide is essential for many of their clients. Their mission is to foster the most successful, well-adjusted generation of wounded service members in our nation's history.
